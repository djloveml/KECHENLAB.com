Product Focus

Designed for controlled lab environments, sample protection, and long-term stable operation.

Shanghai Kechen Experimental Equipment Co., Ltd. focuses on laboratory research and education instruments for biology, chemistry, medical, and agricultural fields. Kechen builds cost-effective domestic laboratory equipment with reliable quality and practical innovation.

Product Lines

Kechen Laboratory Instrument Series

Core series extracted from the product brochure, covering temperature, humidity, light, incubation, drying, vacuum, and shaking-culture applications.

Intelligent Climate Chamber

7-inch touch-screen control with temperature, humidity, and light programming for plant cultivation, seed germination, seedling growth, and microbial culture.

  • 450-1200 L and custom capacities
  • Light intensity up to 30000LX
  • Optional CO2, ventilation, and 4G remote control

Temperature Humidity Chamber

For constant temperature and humidity tests, with 30 programmable time segments and a stainless-steel chamber for stable long-term operation.

  • Temperature range 4-65°C
  • Humidity fluctuation ≤±1.5%RH
  • Operation logs and data records

Light Incubator

Simulates illuminated culture conditions for plant growth, tissue culture, seed germination, microbial storage, and light exposure tests.

  • Stepless light adjustment
  • Microprocessor program control
  • Top or multi-side lighting design

Plant Growth Chamber

Supports multi-segment temperature, humidity, and light programming for plant cultivation, seedling growth, tissue cells, and microbial culture.

  • 24 programmable time segments
  • Dedicated biological light source
  • Optional automatic ventilation

Biochemical Incubator

For environmental protection, hygiene and epidemic prevention, drug testing, aquaculture, BOD testing, microbial culture, and sample storage.

  • 50-350 L capacity range
  • 5-50°C temperature range
  • B/BE glass-door models available

Electric Incubator

For constant-temperature incubation and sample storage in research institutes, medical labs, industrial labs, and portable emergency use.

  • Desktop and portable formats
  • PID control and over-temperature alarm
  • Four-wall surrounding heating

Drying Oven / Vacuum Drying Oven

Vertical and horizontal forced-air drying ovens plus desktop vacuum drying ovens for drying, baking, sterilization, and heat-sensitive materials.

  • Forced-air drying up to RT+250°C
  • High-temperature test chambers up to 500°C
  • Vacuum drying below 130Pa

Shaking Incubator

For bacterial culture, fermentation, hybridization, biochemical reactions, and cell tissue research requiring temperature and shaking control.

  • 0-300rpm shaking frequency
  • Single to three-layer combinations
  • USB data export and power-failure recovery
