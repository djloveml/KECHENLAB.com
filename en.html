Product Focus

Designed around environmental stability, chamber uniformity, and reliable experimental data.

KECHENLAB incubators cover common laboratory needs including constant-temperature incubation, CO2 cell culture, mold and plant culture, and low-temperature biochemical incubation. This portal can serve as a corporate homepage, product landing page, or product catalog entry point.

Constant Temperature Incubator

CO2 Incubator

Built for cell culture and tissue-engineering workflows where CO2 control, humidity, and contamination control matter.

  • Infrared CO2 sensing
  • Rounded stainless-steel chamber
  • Optional high-temperature sterilization
03

Mold Incubator

For mold, microbiology, plant tissue, and environmental test applications requiring coordinated temperature and humidity control.

  • Humidity control module
  • Programmable operation profiles
  • Observation window and lighting
04

Biochemical Incubator

For low-temperature culture, BOD testing, sample storage, and long-duration experiments requiring refrigeration.

  • Low-noise refrigeration system
  • Over-temperature alarm protection
  • Data logging interface
